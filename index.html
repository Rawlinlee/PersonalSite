    Why Double Degree?

    As an INTJ, I've always been naturally curious- which is perhaps why I've always been interested in a wide variety of things, such as drawing/art, design, cooking, natural sciences, human pyschology, technological developments, and of course, programming and yes, business too! As I've developed as a programmer, I've become increasing infatiuated with the way programming was able to bridge my many interests, but perhaps more importantly, how it's been able to mentally stimulate and push me to learn and harden my own logic. It's so incredibly cool and satisfying to be able to struggle with a problem, to expand your technical skills to solve them, and ultimately produce a "virtually tangible" product that you can share and expierence.

    That being said, I look forward to continue to expand my skill sets and truly define who I am and what I enjoy doing. For that reason, I'm also pursing a business adminstration degree. Whilst I may be seemingly treating this as but a side-objective, business in it's own right is a vast, general skill with a wide array of applications. It is a very core part of society as we know it, and as such, I am sure that the skills I've acquired in the process will doubtless be of use- despite what people may generally believe. From the soft-skills alone, I know this won't be an investment I regret!
